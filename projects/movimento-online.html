MovimentoOnline

Dynamic and acessible learning

   The project MovimentoOnline - Aventura para todos is a website to complement the saturday activity of young scouts, aged between 10 and 21 years, guiding the same on reliable plataforms to the development in and out of the scout movement, based on 3 pillars, acessibility, web didatic and the scout method.

   The website adopts the WGAC (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) and the Brazilian Law of Inclusion of the Person with Deficiency (Law 13.146), leading everyone to a equal learning pratice based on the chunking principle and the diffuse learning.

   The project was voluntarily elaborated to achieve the maximum decoration among youngs scout aged 18 to 21 years, the BP Badge. Product of more than 100 hours of certified courses and 7000 lines of programming and markup code.
